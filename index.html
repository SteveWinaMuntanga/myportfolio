Brief History of My Freelancing Journey

I started coding in 2019 whilst in high school. Because I had to focus on my studies, coding was more like a free-time hobby. I had little access to mobile phones, computers and the internet because possession of gadgets was prohibited at our school. There were no ICT subjects for seniors either.

However, things changed in 2021, a year after graduating from high school. I was now free to access technology and the internet. However, I was living in a village without electricity and internet access(internet connection was only available at certain spots). We were using solar energy, thankfully, it was enough to charge my itel A11 which I was using to learn coding. I did not have a PC.

Because internet connection was only available at certain spots, I would go to a nearest spot, open SoloLearn app, and go to my courses and select 'Make available offline'. That helped.

The challenge was that I had to skip exercises that need real-time updates during learning. So I would learn during the day, and go to the spots at night to complete exercises. That was how I obtained my first certificates in HTML, CSS, PHP and SQL.

You have probably noticed that JavaScript is missing on the list of courses. I was learning JavaScript too. If you have taken JavaScript course on SoloLearn, you know that it has alot of exercises and challenges to complete for one to obtain a certificate. That's why I had to skip it.

In the year 2022, everything changed when I moved to Livingstone in search of a better life. I knew I had to move to achieve my dreams. In Livingstone, everything that was making learning difficult is not there. I started learning on W3Schools. But I still use SoloLearn.

I made new friends both offline and online who really inspired me and changed the course of my life. Most of them are from Nigeria, South Africa, Kenya, USA, Philipines, Zambia and India. Most are developers, graphic designers and entrepreneurs. That's when I started my business Wina Mobile and became a freelancer.

I learned more skills including digital marketing, Search Engine Optimization and Phone/PC repair. I am still learning and reading more books. As they say, "When you stop learning, you start dying".
